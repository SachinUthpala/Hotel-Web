3 Bedroom Villa

Blue Haven villa is located in Weligama Bay. It is a charming family beach home that basks in the sunshine perched above a lagoon with seasonal surfing and swimming.

There are 5 bedrooms; 4 in the main house that can be closed off around an inner courtyard making it perfect for families with small children, and the 5th bedroom in a garden suite. The villa is spacious with a front lawn where evenings can be spent, a fully equipped sitting room with DVD players and a surround system. The pool is located on the lower terrace, a little private gate within the villa confines opens up to a lagoon for safe swimming and snorkeling (seasonally). Meals will be taken care of by the in-house chef, serving the best food catered according to the preferences of guests. It is a lovely house perfect for families.
